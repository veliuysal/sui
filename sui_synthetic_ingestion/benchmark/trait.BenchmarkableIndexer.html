Expand description

A trait that can be implemented on top of any indexer to benchmark its throughput. It will generate synthetic transactions and checkpoints as ingestion source.

fn subscribe_to_committed_checkpoints( &self, ) -> Receiver<Option<IndexerProgress>>

Allows the benchmark to subscribe and monitor the committed checkpoints progress. This is needed both in order to log periodic throughput, but also to know when the benchmark can stop.
